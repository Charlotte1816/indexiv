<div class="theorem">

1. Sum of an arithmetic sequence $\{a_n \mid a_k = a_1 + (k-1)d\}$:
    $$
        a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n = \frac{n}{2}\left(2a_1+(n-1)d\right)
    $$

2. Sum of a geometric sequence $\{a_n \mid a_k = a_1 r^{k-1}\}$:
    $$
        a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n = \begin{cases}
            a_1\frac{1-r^n}{1-r} & (r \neq 1)
            \\
            na_1 & (r = 1)
        \end{cases}
    $$

</div>
