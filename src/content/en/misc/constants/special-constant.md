Napier's constant $e$ is defined as
$$
    e = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^n;
$$
$$
\begin{align*}
    e \approx 2.\ &71828\ 18284\ 59045\ 23536\ 02874\ 71352\ 66249\ 77572\ 47093\ 69995
    \\
    &95749\ 66967\ 62772\ 40766\ 30353\ 54759\ 45713\ 82178\ 52516\ 64274
\end{align*}
$$
Euler's constant $\gamma$ is defined as
$$
    \gamma = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{n} - \ln n\right);
$$
$$
\begin{align*}
    \gamma \approx 0.\ &57721\ 56649\ 01532\ 86060\ 65120\ 90082\ 40243\ 10421\ 59335\ 93992
    \\
    &35988\ 05767\ 23488\ 48677\ 26777\ 66467\ 09369\ 47063\ 29174\ 67495
\end{align*}
$$
